    Information Science, Systems, and Technology (ISST) Major in the College of Engineering

    The BS in Information Science, Systems, and Technology (ISST) allows students to study the creation, representation, organization, application, and analysis of information in digital form.

    The ISST major studies the design and management of complex information systems, with an emphasis on information systems engineering in broad application contexts, where issues at the confluence of information science, technology, and management are the primary concerns. Students take classes in operations research modeling techniques, statistics, computer science, economics, and the social and organizational contexts in which transformative information systems exist.
