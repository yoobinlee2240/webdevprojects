    ISST Concentrations

    The College of Engineering's Information Science, Systems, and Technology major offers students the ability to specialize in areas of interest through primary and secondary concentrations. Below, you'll find detailed descriptions of each concentration, including relevant courses and career paths.

    Primary Concentrations

    • Data Science
    • Interactive Technologies
    • Networks, Crowds, and Markets

    Secondary Concentrations

    • Behavioral Science
    • Data Science
    • Digital Culture and Production
    • Information Ethics, Law, and Policy
    • Interactive Technologies
    • Networks, Crowds, and Markets
    • UX (User Experience) Design

    Data Science

    This concentration equips students to learn about the world through data analytics.

    • CS 3780: Machine Learning for Intelligent Systems
    • CS 4786: Machine Learning for Data Science
    • STSCI 3740: Data Mining and Machine Learning
